M. Mesbahuddin Sarker Associate Professor, Institute of Information Technology

PROFILE

SHORT BIOGRAPHY

Obtained B.Sc. and M.Sc. degrees from the Mathematics Department of Jahangirnagar University, Bangladesh. Also completed M.Sc. in Communication Systems from the department of Electrical Engineering and Information Technology, Technical University of Kaiserslautern, Germany. At present pursuing Ph.D. studies at the University of St. Gallen, Switzerland on the field of Electronic Voting (E-Voting) Information Systems.

 

RESEARCH INTEREST

E-Voting, E-Democracy, E-Government, Media and Communication, Digital Technology and Society etc.
